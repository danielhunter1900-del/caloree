Same Home tab, but now showing the plan side. The pill flips to "Back to tracker" (dark, not purple). Tab bar highlight subtly tints purple to signal you're in planner mode. One tap, no context loss — both screens share the same day header.

Behaviour

Home tab is the only tab affected. Log, Trends, Profile are unchanged. Opening the app defaults to whichever view you last used; resets to Tracker each morning. The switch is a single animated transition (slide-left / slide-right) — takes ~200ms, keeps scroll position per view.

Rest days: planner shows "🌙 Rest day · log light moves if you want" with the Start button hidden. Days with no plan session show "Make it up" suggesting a replacement from your preferences.

Adaptive notifications (e.g. Sunday's weekly recompute) open Caloree and land you directly on the planner view with the proposal surfaced — no mode switching needed.

Why not a tab? You said you don't want the extra tab; a bottom switch keeps the 4-tab layout and makes the relationship between the two views explicit — tracker and planner are two sides of today, not separate places.
